CTRLit Help

How to Use

  • Touchpad: Drag your finger or mouse on the touchpad area to move the cursor on the desktop.
  • Left Click: Tap the "Left Click" button to perform a left mouse click.
  • Right Click: Tap the "Right Click" button to perform a right mouse click.
  • Keyboard: Tap the "Keyboard" button to show/hide the virtual keyboard.

Troubleshooting

  • Connection Issues: If the status indicator is red, you're not connected. Try refreshing the page.
  • Motion Sensors: If motion sensors aren't working, your device may not support them. Use the touchpad instead.
  • Lag: If there's lag, try moving your finger more slowly on the touchpad.
